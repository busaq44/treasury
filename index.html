TREASURY HEADQUARTERS, BAYELSA STATE

Welcome to Treasury Headquarters

The Treasury Headquarters is headed by the Accountant-General of the State and some of whose responsibilities as contained in the Financial Regulation (2009) are:

Financial Regulations

Maintain and operates the State account and provide financial regulations as at when due

Manages the investments

Manages the investments; Serve as Chief Accounting Officer of the Receipts and Payments

Financial Statements

Collate, present and publish statutory financial statement of the State Government

History of Treasury Headquarters

The Bayelsa State Treasury department which is an offshoot of the Rivers State Treasury is under the Ministry of Finance. It came into being on the 1st of October 1996 when the State (Bayelsa) was created by the then Head of State, Late Gen. Sani Abacha.
